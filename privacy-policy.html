Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how gAIns ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account information — name and email address when you create an account
  • Health and fitness data — workout history, exercise sets, personal records, body weight, height, body fat percentage, and training goals you enter
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app to improve features
  • Device information — device type and operating system for compatibility
  • Payment information — subscription status (payment details are handled by Stripe and never stored by us)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the gAIns service
  • To power AI coaching features with context from your training data
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To send important account and service notifications
  • To improve app features and performance

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following service providers necessary to operate gAIns:

  • Supabase — database hosting and user authentication
  • Anthropic — AI coaching (your prompts and workout context are sent to generate responses)
  • Stripe — subscription payment processing

Each provider is bound by their own privacy policy and data processing agreements.

4. Health Data

Workout, body composition, and performance data you enter is used solely to provide the gAIns service. It is not shared with advertisers, sold to third parties, or used for any purpose other than operating the app.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from within the app (Profile → Delete Account) or by submitting a request at akinyavuz1.github.io/gains-delete. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of the request.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS) and Supabase Row Level Security to ensure your data can only be accessed by you. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

7. Children's Privacy

gAIns is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of gAIns after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at: privacy@gains.app
